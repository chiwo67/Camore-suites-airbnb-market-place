Locate us at:

SmartHomes is nestled in the leafy, upscale neighbourhood of Kilimani — one of Nairobi's most sought-after residential areas. Getting here from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) is quick and easy! By Uber (Recommended) Simply open your Uber app upon arrival at JKIA and search for SmartHomes, Kilimani. The ride typically takes 20 to 40 minutes depending on traffic, and is a comfortable, affordable, and hassle-free way to reach the property. Nairobi traffic can be busy during peak hours (7 to 9am and 5 to 7pm), so factor that in if you're arriving at those times. About the Neighbourhood Kilimani is a vibrant, safe, and well-connected part of Nairobi. You'll find yourself surrounded by great restaurants, cafés, supermarkets, and shopping centres — everything you need is just minutes away. It's also centrally located, making it easy to explore the rest of the city. Pro Tip 🗺️ If your Uber driver needs help locating the property, feel free to reach out to me directly — I'm always available and happy to guide you or your driver to the door!
