Getting to Tatu City from JKIA 🛬 Tatu City is a surprisingly easy and straightforward journey from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport — making it the perfect destination whether you're arriving for business, leisure, or to explore your future home. By Uber (Recommended) Once you land at JKIA, simply open your Uber app and search for Tatu City, Ruiru. The drive covers approximately 19 miles and takes around 32 minutes under normal traffic conditions. It's the most comfortable and hassle-free option, especially if you're travelling with luggage. Bear in mind that Nairobi traffic can be heavier during peak hours (7 to 9am and 5 to 7pm), so allow extra time if arriving during those windows. Rome2Rio By Bus / Matatu You can also take a bus from JKIA, connecting via key stages in the city centre, with a total journey time of around 1 hour 42 minutes. This is a budget-friendly option for the adventurous traveller who wants to experience the city along the way. Rome2Rio The Route From JKIA, your driver will head north through Nairobi city, joining the Thika Superhighway — one of Kenya's busiest and most modern expressways. Tatu City benefits from excellent connectivity via the Thika Superhighway and Eastern Bypass, keeping Nairobi's city centre easily accessible at all times. Tatu City is located in Ruiru, Kiambu County, just beyond the northern edge of Nairobi. BuyRentKenya Pro Tip 🗺️ If your driver is unfamiliar with the exact location within Tatu City, specify your destination — whether it's Kijani Ridge, Unity Homes, or another development — as the city spans a large 5,000-acre area.
